Best Movies According to Bhavya

My Top 3 favourite movies of all-time

Godavari

On a cruise boat on the River Godavari, a romance between an idealistic young man, Ram, and an independent young woman, Sita, blossoms.

Anand

This film has the lots of freshness and is a class apart.

Seetharamam

An orphan soldier, Lieutenant Ram's life changes, after he gets a letter from a girl named Sita. He meets her and love blossoms between them.
